THE RULE OF THREES

You can survive roughly:

  • 3 Minutes without breathable air.
  • 3 Hours in extreme environment (cold/heat).
  • 3 Days without water.
  • 3 Weeks without food.
S.T.O.P. WHEN LOST

S - Stop. Don't panic. Stay put.

T - Think. What do you have? Who knows where you are?

O - Observe. Weather, terrain, resources.

P - Plan. Prioritize shelter, water, and fire.

WATER PURIFICATION

1. Boil: Bring to rolling boil for 1 min (3 mins at high alt).

2. Solar Still: Dig a hole, place container in center, cover hole with plastic sheet, place rock over sheet center above container. Sun evaporates soil moisture.

SEVERE BLEEDING

Apply direct, continuous pressure with a clean cloth. Elevate wound above heart. Do NOT remove soaked bandages; add more on top. Tourniquet only as absolute last resort for limbs.
